About Restro

Our Story

Restro was founded with a simple mission: to bring authentic, delicious cuisine to our community while maintaining the highest standards of quality and hospitality.

Started in 2015 by culinary enthusiasts with over 40 years of combined experience in fine dining, Restro has grown from a small neighborhood bistro to one of the city's most celebrated restaurants. Our commitment to sourcing the finest local ingredients and our passion for innovative yet traditional cooking methods set us apart.

Today, we continue to serve our guests with the same dedication and love that started it all, earning us multiple awards and the loyalty of thousands of satisfied customers.
